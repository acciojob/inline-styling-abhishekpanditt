How many Flowers are there in the world? There are over 400,000 flowering plant species across the world, producing a variety of types of flowers in a range of colors and shades and in all different shapes and sizes. No matter your taste or style, you're sure to find a flower that suits you and your loved ones best

Roses pictures

Rose belongs to the family Rosaceae and genus Rosa; the latter contains about 150 species. According to Greek mythology, Aphrodite, goddess of beauty, gave the rose its name in honor of her son Eros by rearranging just one letter in his name.
